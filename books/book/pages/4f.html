"There was a cholera outbreak and the hospital was dirty. I had an idea that the dirty state of the hospital was helping the disease spread among the patients. Well, I don’t mind speaking up and getting my opinion heard: you can’t be too shy in life Major. I suggested lots of things that I thought would improve the hospital. A lot of my suggestions were adopted and conditions and the death rate improved. This only made me more convinced I was doing the right thing and moving in the right direction with my life."

Then the war started. And everything changed… For all of us.

I was called up to fight and Florence was now faced with the biggest challenge of her career.
