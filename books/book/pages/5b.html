Iwas to lead a unit of one hundred soldiers of the British Army in what was now being called The Crimean War. Crimea was part of Russia… (little bit more detail about the war and its causes here…) and Britain would be fighting with France and Turkey against Russia. Thousands of us sailed to Crimea, to the Black Sea.

It wasn't easy. Of course there was the battles but I don’t want to think about them. As well as that: supplies ran out; the food was dreadful; there was little medicine and no clean clothing and bedding. By the following year 18,000 soldiers were admitted to military "hospitals".
