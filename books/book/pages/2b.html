It was so dark all I could see was the outline of bodies lying in beds. I could see some of their chests moving up and down... I couldn't tell if the other bodies were still breathing.

I had not slept for hours, maybe even days: such was the pain from my injuries. I was cut, bruised and I had broken bones. It's hard to sleep when every time you move you feel a bolt of pain shoot through your body. Even when you lie still, the pain throbs like a hot light, slowly being turned on and off. The pain makes you feel sick... I was exhausted.

My eyes just wanted to fall shut and every time they did the comforting blanket of sleep would roll from my closed eyes, to the front of my brain and gently sweep to the back... wonderful sleep... a medicine that would ease my pain and exhaustion. But it was brief. Soon a cough or a groan, would wake me and my pain and I would be reunited.
