She had become admired by many of the public: more importantly than all the songs and poems written about her, she had managed, in only a few years, to turn nursing from a disrespected job into a respectable, honourable vocation that even wealthy women wanted to work in.

I saw the improvements in the hospital I had to attend for my checkups. Everything was so much cleaner and the nurses were proud to work there. Hospitals were finally places that were clean, spacious and where you felt you might get healthy in. All thanks to Florence.
