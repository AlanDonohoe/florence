Once she had read through my notes she put them back and produced a damp cloth from her kit. She carefully wiped clean my face and then replaced the bandage on my leg with a clean one.

"Good night Major. Try and get some sleep."

"Good night Florence. Thank you."

She picked up her lamp and moved to the injured soldier in the next bed. I fell back into darkness again and I think I finally, briefly, did get some sleep.
