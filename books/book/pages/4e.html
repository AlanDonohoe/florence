After Italy I traveled to Germany to visit a church group who were helping the sick and poor: which just confirmed my dedication to become a nurse. I had four months of training and studied and wrote about the training they gave the church members who were helping the sick. It became the first thing I had published. Now I was ready to get to work.”

She took a job at a hospital in London and was working there for a year when she was called up to lead a team of almost forty nurses to the war in Crimea to look after the injured British soldiers. Which is where she is now… sitting talking to me.
